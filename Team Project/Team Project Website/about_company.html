The Trailblazers Story
Our company was founded in the year 2020 by a group of ten enthusiastic students who were determined to bring a change through their bright minds. The initial idea for building the company was introduced by their professor Mr. Baidurya Bhattacharya. It was the immense hard work and dedication put by these students into their company because of which it was able to actually compete with the real world and be in the position it is today. A trailblazer means someone who is the first to do something, an innovator. This was the perfect term to describe our founders. So, this is how our company got its name ‘Trailblazers’.
Our Values and Behaviours
We aim to conduct our businesses ethically and in a manner that is respectful to all. We achieve this by aligning our goals with our six core values.
Excellence:
We are committed to excellence, in spirit and action. We believe everything that we do and everything we think can always get better. We see all of our activities in terms of our higher purpose and ideals, which drives our quest for excellence always.
Respect:
We believe that without respecting all our customers and clients there can be no Trailblazers. We acknowledge that there may be a difference of perspectives but there must always be respect.
Ownership Mindset:
We believe the success and reputation of the company is paramount. Having an ownership mind-set is fundamental to our existence. It ensures our strong commitment to the highest standards of safety and environment.
One Team:
Whatever the strength of the individual, we will accomplish more together. We put the team ahead of our personal success and commit to building its capability. We trust each other to deliver on our respective obligations.
Integrity:
Upholding our reputation is paramount as we are judged by how we act. We are committed to be truthful in all our actions. We respect the world in which we operate. It begins with compliance with laws and regulations. We hold ourselves to the highest ethical standards and behave in ways that earn the trust of others.
Customer Value:
We believe the customer is the reason for our existence and the only guarantee to our future. Everything that we do must delight our customer, each time and always.
Leadership and team:
Our company’s leadership team is passionate about providing quality service and innovative products. We foster a work environment that is committed to the task at hand and excel at seeking out professionals that take pride in the challenge to complete projects above client expectations.
